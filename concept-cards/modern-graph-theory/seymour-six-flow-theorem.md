# Quick Definition
Seymour proved that every bridgeless graph has a nowhere-zero 6-flow, the best known result toward Tutte's 5-flow conjecture.

# Core Definition
"Seymour proved that, in fact, every bridgeless graph has a nowhere-zero 6-flow" (Bollobás, p. 355). This improved Jaeger's 1979 result of 8-flows but remains short of Tutte's conjectured 5-flows.
