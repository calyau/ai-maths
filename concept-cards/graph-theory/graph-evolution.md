# Quick Definition
As p grows from 0, a random graph acquires structure in sudden jumps: first edges (p ~ n^{-2}), first cycles (p ~ n^{-1}), connectivity (p ~ (log n)/n), Hamiltonicity (p ~ (1+eps)(log n)/n).

# Core Definition
As p grows, G in G(n,p) acquires structure in sudden jumps: from p ~ n^{-2}, components with > 2 vertices; around p ~ n^{-1}, first cycles; around p ~ (log n)n^{-1}, connectivity; at p ~ (1+epsilon)(log n)n^{-1}, Hamilton cycles (Diestel, p. 315).
