# Quick Definition
A cylinder is the product space S^1 x [0,1] or any space homeomorphic to it. It serves as the strip neighbourhood of a two-sided circle on a surface.

# Core Definition
"A cylinder is the product space S^1 x [0,1], or any space homeomorphic to it. Its middle circle is the circle S^1 x {1/2}" (Diestel, p. 362).

# Prerequisites
- **Surface**

# Key Properties
1. Removing the middle circle yields two components (two-sided)
2. Used as strip neighbourhood for two-sided circles
3. Added between boundary circles when constructing a handle

# Relationships
## Related
- **Handle** -- A handle is attached via a cylinder
