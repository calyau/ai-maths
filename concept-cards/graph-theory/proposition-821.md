# Quick Definition
Every infinite connected graph has a vertex of infinite degree or contains a ray (Proposition 8.2.1). This is a consequence of König's infinity lemma.

# Core Definition
**Proposition 8.2.1**: Every infinite connected graph has a vertex of infinite degree or contains a ray (Diestel, p. 214). The proof applies König's infinity lemma to distance layers from a fixed vertex.
