# Quick Definition
The adhesion of a tree-decomposition at an edge t1t2 of T is the set V_{t1} cap V_{t2}. Adhesion sets are the separators in the decomposition.

# Core Definition
The *adhesion* at an edge t1t2 of T in a tree-decomposition (T, V) is V_{t1} cap V_{t2}. By Lemma 12.3.1, adhesion sets separate the graph: V_{t1} cap V_{t2} separates the parts on either side of the edge (Diestel, pp. 330-331).
