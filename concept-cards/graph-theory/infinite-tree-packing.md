# Quick Definition
Theorem 8.5.10: A locally finite multigraph has k edge-disjoint topological spanning trees iff every finite vertex partition into l sets has at least k(l-1) cross-edges.

# Core Definition
**Theorem 8.5.10**: For all k and locally finite multigraphs G: (i) G has k edge-disjoint topological spanning trees iff (ii) for every finite partition into l sets, G has >= k(l-1) cross-edges (Diestel, p. 245).
