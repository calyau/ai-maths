# Quick Definition
A Mobius strip is the space obtained from the square [0,1] x [0,1] by identifying (1,y) with (0,1-y). It serves as the strip neighbourhood of a one-sided circle on a surface.

# Core Definition
"A Mobius strip is any space homeomorphic to the product space [0,1] x [0,1] after identification of (1,y) with (0,1-y) for all y in [0,1]" (Diestel, p. 362).

# Prerequisites
- **Surface**

# Key Properties
1. Removing the middle circle leaves ONE component (one-sided)
2. Used as strip neighbourhood for one-sided circles
3. Non-orientable
4. Appears in surfaces with crosscaps

# Relationships

## Contrasts With
