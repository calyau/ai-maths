# Quick Definition
Tree-width has a game-theoretic characterization: tw(G) + 1 equals the minimum number of cops needed to catch a robber on G, where cops can teleport but the robber sees their moves.

# Core Definition
The tree-width of G plus 1 equals the minimum number of cops needed to catch a robber on G in the following game: cops announce positions, then teleport; robber sees their plan and moves along any path avoiding cops. This connects to brambles (Diestel, notes to Section 12.3).
