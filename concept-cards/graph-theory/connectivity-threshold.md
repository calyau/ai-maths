# Quick Definition
For constant p in (0,1), almost every G(n,p) is k-connected for any fixed k. This follows from P_{2,k-1}: any two vertices have a common neighbour outside any k-1 vertices.

# Core Definition
**Corollary 11.3.3**: For every constant p in (0,1) and k in N, almost every graph in G(n,p) is k-connected. Proof: every graph in P_{2,k-1} is k-connected (Diestel, p. 313).
