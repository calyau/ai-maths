# Quick Definition
Every maximal planar graph with at least 4 vertices is 3-connected (Corollary 4.4.7). This connects the density of planar graphs to their connectivity.

# Core Definition
**Corollary 4.4.7**: "Every maximal planar graph with at least four vertices is 3-connected" (Diestel, p. 101).

# Prerequisites
- **Maximal planar graph**, **Kuratowski's theorem**

# Key Properties
1. Follows from Lemma 4.4.5 and Theorem 4.4.6
2. Combined with Whitney's theorem: maximal planar graphs have unique embeddings
3. The 3-connected structure ensures "nice" face boundaries
