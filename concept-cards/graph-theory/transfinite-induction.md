# Quick Definition
A proof/construction technique for infinite graphs: proceed step by step indexed by ordinals, making choices at successor steps and taking limits at limit ordinals.

# Core Definition
Transfinite induction and recursion proceed step by step indexed by ordinals rather than natural numbers. At successor steps, one extends the construction; at limit ordinals, one takes unions. The challenge is ensuring the structure is correct at limit steps (Diestel, pp. 208-210).
