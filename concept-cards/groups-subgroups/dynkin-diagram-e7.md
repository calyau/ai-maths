# Quick Definition

E_7 is an exceptional Dynkin diagram with 7 nodes: a chain of 6 nodes with one branch from the 4th node. It has 126 roots, and P/Q ≅ ℤ/2ℤ.

# Core Definition

**E_7** is the exceptional Dynkin diagram with 7 nodes: a chain of 6 with a branch from the 4th (Milne, §1j, p. 304).

# Prerequisites

- **Dynkin diagram** — E_7 is one of the exceptional types

# Key Properties

1. Rank: 7; Number of roots: 126
2. P/Q ≅ ℤ/2ℤ (from table on p. 376)
3. Simply-laced (all roots same length)
