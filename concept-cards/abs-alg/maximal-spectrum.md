# Quick Definition
The maximal spectrum mSpec R is the set of all maximal ideals of R. Over algebraically closed fields, mSpec k[V] corresponds to the points of V via the Nullstellensatz.

# Core Definition
The set of all maximal ideals of R, denoted **mSpec R**, is called the maximal spectrum (Definition, p. 736). Maximal ideals are the closed points in Spec R. For k[V] over algebraically closed k, mSpec k[V] bijects with points of V by the Nullstellensatz.
