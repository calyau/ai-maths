# Competency Questions for "Abstract Algebra" (Dummit & Foote, 3rd ed.)

## Definitional (What is X?)
1. What is a group?
2. What is a ring?
3. What is a field?
4. What is an ideal?
5. What is a module?
6. What is a vector space?
7. What is a homomorphism (group, ring, module)?
8. What is an isomorphism?
9. What is a normal subgroup?
10. What is a quotient group (quotient ring, quotient module)?
11. What is a Sylow p-subgroup?
12. What is a principal ideal domain (PID)?
13. What is a unique factorization domain (UFD)?
14. What is a Euclidean domain?
15. What is a field extension?
16. What is the Galois group of an extension?
17. What is a splitting field?
18. What is a Noetherian ring?
19. What is an algebraic closure?
20. What is a representation of a finite group?

## Relational (How does X relate to Y?)
1. How do normal subgroups relate to quotient groups?
2. How does the kernel of a homomorphism relate to normal subgroups?
3. How do Euclidean domains, PIDs, and UFDs relate to each other?
4. How does the Galois group relate to the structure of field extensions?
5. How do modules generalize vector spaces?
6. How do ideals in rings relate to normal subgroups in groups?
7. How does localization relate to the prime spectrum of a ring?
8. How do characters relate to representations?
9. How do free modules relate to projective modules?
10. How does the Fundamental Theorem of Galois Theory connect subgroups and intermediate fields?

## Procedural (How do I do X?)
1. How do I determine whether a subset is a subgroup?
2. How do I construct a quotient group?
3. How do I apply the Sylow theorems to analyze group structure?
4. How do I determine whether a polynomial is irreducible?
5. How do I compute the Galois group of a polynomial?
6. How do I find the rational canonical form of a linear transformation?
7. How do I find the Jordan canonical form of a matrix?
8. How do I compute a Gröbner basis?
9. How do I determine the splitting field of a polynomial?
10. How do I compute the character table of a finite group?

## Prerequisite (What must I know before X?)
1. What must I know before understanding quotient groups?
2. What must I know before learning the Sylow theorems?
3. What must I know before studying Galois theory?
4. What must I know before studying module theory?
5. What must I know before studying homological algebra?
6. What must I know before studying algebraic geometry?
7. What must I know before studying representation theory?
8. What must I know before understanding tensor products?

## Diagnostic (What distinguishes X from Y?)
1. What distinguishes a group from a monoid?
2. What distinguishes a ring from a field?
3. What distinguishes a PID from a general integral domain?
4. What distinguishes a normal extension from a separable extension?
5. What distinguishes a direct product from a semidirect product?
6. What distinguishes a free module from a projective module?
7. What distinguishes the rational canonical form from the Jordan canonical form?
8. What distinguishes an Artinian ring from a Noetherian ring?
9. What distinguishes a prime ideal from a maximal ideal?
10. What distinguishes a Galois extension from a general field extension?
