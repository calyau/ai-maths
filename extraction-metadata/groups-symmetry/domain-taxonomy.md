# Domain Taxonomy for Groups and Symmetry (Armstrong, 1988)

## Categories
- fundamentals: Basic group theory definitions, axioms, and elementary properties
- number-groups: Groups arising from number systems (integers, rationals, reals, complex)
- symmetry-groups: Dihedral groups, symmetry groups of solids, rotation groups
- permutation-groups: Symmetric groups, alternating groups, cycle structure, sign
- matrix-groups: General linear, orthogonal, special orthogonal, unitary groups
- subgroup-theory: Subgroups, generators, cyclic groups, Lagrange's theorem, cosets
- structure-theory: Direct products, semidirect products, classification results
- equivalence-partitions: Equivalence relations, partitions, congruence classes
- normal-subgroups-quotients: Normal subgroups, quotient groups, isomorphism theorems
- homomorphisms: Homomorphisms, kernels, images, isomorphism theorems
- conjugacy: Conjugacy classes, centre, centraliser
- group-actions: Actions, orbits, stabilizers, counting theorem
- finite-group-classification: Cauchy's theorem, Sylow theorems, group classification
- abelian-groups: Finitely generated abelian groups, torsion coefficients, rank
- automorphisms: Automorphism groups, inner automorphisms, semidirect products
- euclidean-geometry: Euclidean group, isometries, translations, rotations, reflections
- crystallography: Lattices, point groups, wallpaper groups, crystallographic restriction
- combinatorial-group-theory: Free groups, presentations, trees, Nielsen-Schreier theorem

## Tiers
- foundational: Chapters 1-4 (symmetry, axioms, number groups, dihedral groups)
- intermediate: Chapters 5-16 (subgroups, permutations, isomorphisms, matrix groups, products, Lagrange, partitions, Cauchy, conjugacy, quotient groups, homomorphisms)
- advanced: Chapters 17-28 (actions, counting, rotation groups, Sylow, abelian classification, row/column ops, automorphisms, Euclidean group, lattices, wallpaper, free groups, trees)

## Notation Conventions
- Group operation: multiplicative notation (xy), except for abelian groups where additive notation (x + y) is used
- Identity element: e (multiplicative), 0 (additive)
- Inverse: x^{-1} (multiplicative), -x (additive)
- Cyclic group: Z_n
- Dihedral group: D_n (order 2n)
- Symmetric group: S_n
- Alternating group: A_n
- General linear group: GL_n
- Orthogonal group: O_n
- Special orthogonal group: SO_n
- Subgroup notation: H < G
- Normal subgroup: H ◁ G
- Isomorphism: G ≅ G'
- Order of group: |G|
- Order of element: smallest positive m with x^m = e
- Coset: gH (left), Hg (right)
- Quotient group: G/H
- Direct product: G × H
- Semidirect product: H ×_φ J
- Free group: F(X), F_n
- Presentation: {generators | relations}
