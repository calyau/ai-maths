# Domain Taxonomy for Abstract Algebra: Theory and Applications

## Categories

- **foundations**: Sets, logic, proofs, mathematical induction, equivalence relations, basic number theory
- **group-theory**: Groups, subgroups, group operations, group properties, group examples
- **cyclic-groups**: Cyclic subgroups, generators, orders of elements
- **permutation-groups**: Symmetric groups, cycle notation, dihedral groups, alternating groups
- **group-structure**: Cosets, Lagrange's theorem, normal subgroups, factor groups, group actions, Sylow theorems, direct products, solvable groups, finite abelian groups
- **morphisms**: Homomorphisms, isomorphisms, automorphisms, kernels, images
- **matrix-groups**: Matrix groups (GL, SL, O, SO), symmetry groups
- **applications-crypto**: Cryptography (private key, public key, RSA)
- **applications-coding**: Error-detecting codes, error-correcting codes, linear codes, polynomial codes
- **ring-theory**: Rings, subrings, ring homomorphisms, ideals, quotient rings, integral domains, division rings
- **polynomial-theory**: Polynomial rings, division algorithm for polynomials, irreducible polynomials, factorization
- **domain-theory**: Fields of fractions, Euclidean domains, PIDs, UFDs, factorization in integral domains
- **lattice-theory**: Lattices, Boolean algebras, electrical circuits
- **linear-algebra**: Vector spaces, subspaces, linear independence, bases, dimension
- **field-theory**: Extension fields, splitting fields, algebraic extensions, finite fields, geometric constructions
- **galois-theory**: Field automorphisms, Galois groups, fundamental theorem, solvability by radicals

## Tiers

- **foundational**: Chapters 1-3 (Preliminaries, Integers, basic Groups)
- **intermediate**: Chapters 4-12, 16, 19-20 (Cyclic groups through Matrix groups, Rings, Lattices, Vector spaces)
- **advanced**: Chapters 13-15, 17-18, 21-23 (Structure of groups, Sylow, Polynomials, Integral domains, Fields, Finite fields, Galois theory)

## Notation Conventions

- Sets: capital letters (A, B, S), standard sets (Z, Q, R, C)
- Groups: (G, *) or just G when operation is clear
- Integers mod n: Z_n
- Subgroups: H <= G (subgroup), H <| G (normal subgroup)
- Cosets: aH (left coset), Ha (right coset)
- Factor groups: G/H
- Homomorphisms: phi: G -> H
- Kernels: ker(phi)
- Images: phi(G) or im(phi)
- Rings: (R, +, *)
- Ideals: I <| R
- Polynomial rings: R[x]
- Field extensions: F(alpha), [E:F] for degree
- Galois group: Gal(E/F)
- Order of element: |a| or ord(a)
- Order of group: |G|
