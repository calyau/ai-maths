## Notation Conventions

### Groups
- G, H, N for algebraic groups
- G(R) for R-points of G
- G^K for base change of G to K
- G^0 or G° for identity component
- R_u(G) for unipotent radical
- D(G) for derived group
- Z(G) for centre

### Algebras
- k for ground field, R for commutative k-algebra
- O(G) for coordinate ring / representing algebra
- Lie(G) or g for Lie algebra of G
- k^sep for separable closure, k^al for algebraic closure

### Tori and Characters
- X*(T) for character group
- X_*(T) for cocharacter group
- ⟨,⟩ for pairing X*(T) × X_*(T) → Z

### Root Systems
- Φ or R for root system
- α^∨ for coroot
- W for Weyl group
- s_α for reflection corresponding to root α
- Δ or S for base/simple roots

### Standard Groups
- GL_n, SL_n, PGL_n
- SO_n, Sp_n, O_n
- G_a (additive), G_m (multiplicative)
- T_n (upper triangular), U_n (upper unitriangular)
- D_n (diagonal)
