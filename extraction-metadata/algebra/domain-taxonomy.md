# Domain Taxonomy for Artin's "Algebra" (2nd Edition)

## Categories

- **matrices**: Matrix operations, row reduction, determinants, permutations
- **group-theory**: Groups, subgroups, homomorphisms, cosets, quotient groups, Sylow theory, free groups
- **linear-algebra**: Vector spaces, bases, dimension, linear transformations, eigenvalues, Jordan form
- **applications-of-linear-algebra**: Orthogonal matrices, rotations, differential equations, matrix exponential
- **symmetry**: Geometric symmetry, isometries, crystallographic groups, group actions, orbits, counting
- **bilinear-forms**: Bilinear, symmetric, Hermitian, skew-symmetric forms; spectral theorem; conics/quadrics
- **linear-groups**: Classical matrix groups (GL, SL, O, SO, U, SU, Sp), Lie algebras, one-parameter groups
- **group-representations**: Representations, characters, irreducibility, Schur's lemma, orthogonality relations
- **ring-theory**: Rings, ideals, quotient rings, polynomial rings, fractions, algebraic geometry
- **factorization**: UFDs, Euclidean domains, Gauss's lemma, irreducibility criteria, Gaussian integers
- **number-theory**: Algebraic integers, quadratic number fields, ideal factorization, class groups, lattices
- **module-theory**: Modules, free modules, Smith normal form, Noetherian rings, structure theorem
- **field-theory**: Field extensions, algebraic/transcendental elements, finite fields, constructibility
- **galois-theory**: Symmetric functions, Galois groups, fundamental theorem, solvability by radicals

## Tiers

- **foundational**: Chapters 1-3 (matrices, basic groups, vector spaces) — entry-level concepts
- **intermediate**: Chapters 4-6, 8, 11 (linear operators, symmetry, bilinear forms, rings) — requires foundational
- **advanced**: Chapters 7, 9-10, 12-16 (Sylow theory, Lie algebras, representations, factoring, fields, Galois) — requires intermediate

## Notation Conventions

### Matrices
- Matrices in uppercase bold or uppercase italic: A, B, M
- Matrix entries: a_{ij}
- Identity matrix: I or I_n
- Transpose: A^t
- Inverse: A^{-1}

### Groups
- Groups in uppercase: G, H, N
- Group operation: juxtaposition (ab) or explicit (a * b)
- Identity element: e or 1
- Subgroup: H ≤ G or H < G (proper)
- Normal subgroup: N ⊲ G
- Quotient group: G/N
- Order: |G|, ord(g)

### Linear Algebra
- Vector spaces: V, W
- Fields: F, k
- Real numbers: ℝ
- Complex numbers: ℂ
- Integers: ℤ
- Dimension: dim V
- Kernel: ker φ
- Image: im φ

### Ring Theory
- Rings: R, S
- Ideals: I, J, (a), (a, b)
- Polynomial ring: R[x], F[x]
- Quotient ring: R/I

### Field Extensions
- Field extension: K/F or L/K
- Degree: [K:F]
- Galois group: Gal(K/F)
- Algebraic closure: F̄
