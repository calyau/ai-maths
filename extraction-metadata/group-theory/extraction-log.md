## Notation Conventions

### Group Operations
- Multiplicative notation by default: ab, a⁻¹
- Additive notation for commutative groups: a + b, -a, na
- Identity element: e or 1

### Subgroups and Quotients
- H ≤ G: H is a subgroup of G
- N ⊲ G: N is normal in G
- G/N: quotient group
- ⟨X⟩: subgroup generated by set X

### Group Actions
- g · x or gx: action of g on x
- Gx₀: orbit of x₀
- Stab(x₀) or G_{x₀}: stabilizer of x₀
- (G : H): index of H in G

### Isomorphisms and Maps
- ≈: isomorphic
- ≃: canonically isomorphic
- Ker(φ), Im(φ): kernel and image
- Aut(G), Inn(G): automorphism groups

### Special Groups
- Sₙ: symmetric group on n letters
- Aₙ: alternating group
- Dₙ: dihedral group of order 2n
- Cₙ or ℤ/nℤ: cyclic group of order n
- GLₙ(F): general linear group
- Q: quaternion group
- Fₓ: free group on set X

### Series and Subgroup Chains
- G ⊃ G₁ ⊃ ... ⊃ {1}: descending chain
- G⁽ⁿ⁾: nth derived subgroup
- Zⁿ(G): nth term of upper central series

### Representation Theory
- χ: character
- F[G]: group algebra
- Mₙ(F): n×n matrix algebra over F
- ⟨χ,ψ⟩: character inner product
